Home

"Hello World"

Welcome to the landing page of my first portfolio. Right now it is still a work in progress, I do plan on adding to it as I go. My website tells a story about myself, my professional experience and my personal journey as a developer.

This site was constructed using modern front end development tools such as Bootstrap 3.0, Gulp, SASS and many more to come! The overall experience that I am going for is a clean, sleek and overall professional appearance.

I have tailored my portfolio around my visions and values. Throughout my story I have developed many skills and built many relationships, as you can see I have background images of my family portraits to highlight my devotion as a great father, husband and overall family man.
